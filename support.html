Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pray the Rosary with Vespers?

Tap the Rosary tab to begin a guided session. The app walks you through each bead with the mystery meditation and prayer text. Use the controls at the bottom to advance through each prayer.

How do I set a daily reminder?

Go to the Journey tab and enable "Daily Reminder" in the settings section. Vespers will send a local notification to remind you to pray each day.

Is my data stored online?

No. All prayer history, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does Vespers work offline?

Yes. All prayers, scripture verses, and mystery content are bundled in the app. No internet connection is needed.

What is the Fatima Prayer option?

The Fatima Prayer ("O my Jesus, forgive us our sins...") can optionally be included after each decade of the Rosary. Toggle it on or off in the Journey tab settings.
