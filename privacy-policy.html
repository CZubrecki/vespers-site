Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

Vespers ("the App") is a Catholic Rosary and daily prayer app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Collection

Vespers does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.

  • Prayer history and streaks are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • User preferences (reminder settings, haptic feedback, Fatima Prayer toggle) are stored locally via UserDefaults.
  • Local notifications are scheduled on-device if you enable Daily Reminders. No notification data leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Vespers does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

Internet Access

Vespers does not require an internet connection to function. All prayer content, scripture verses, and mystery data are bundled within the app.

Children's Privacy

Vespers does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by removing the App. No server-side data exists to delete.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via our support page.
